About the Firm

Merchant Banking
with Conviction

Core Capital Partners invests in entrepreneurs pursuing high-growth concepts across healthcare technology, fintech, strategic metals, clean technology, and plant-based alternatives. Our merchant banking approach encompasses capital raising, deal structuring, and advisory services designed to build durable businesses.

Since 2005, we have raised, invested, and co-invested over $540 million. Our portfolio companies have achieved a peak aggregate market value of $3.4 billion, reflecting our commitment to identifying and supporting transformative opportunities across our four core verticals.

Headquartered in Vancouver, British Columbia, Core Capital Partners maintains deep relationships with institutional and accredited investors, enabling long-term capital deployment and strategic portfolio management. The firm is led by Kam Thindal, Managing Partner, who has directed the firm's investment strategy since inception.

Our Process

Investment Approach

A disciplined, four-stage methodology applied consistently across all verticals and market conditions.

01

Discovery

Systematic identification of high-growth opportunities through proprietary deal flow, sector research, and strategic referral networks. Early-stage evaluation filters for market timing, team quality, and capital efficiency.

02

Due Diligence

Comprehensive analysis of financial, legal, operational, and market fundamentals. Independent verification of management claims, competitive positioning, and regulatory compliance across all jurisdictions.

03

Structuring

Custom deal architecture that aligns investor interests, management incentives, and capital requirements. Governance frameworks, milestone-based tranching, and downside protection built into every transaction.

04

Execution

Active portfolio management through board participation, capital markets advisory, and operational support. Ongoing monitoring, strategic guidance, and follow-on capital coordination through each stage of growth.
